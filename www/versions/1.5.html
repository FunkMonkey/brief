What's new in Brief 1.5?

Brief is now compatible with Firefox 4.

New look and improved interface:

  • All items folder at the top of the sidebar.
  • Feeds are not paginated, items are loaded as the user scrolls down ("infinite scrolling").
  • Rearranged and simplified toolbar.
  • New shortcuts to mark all items on the screen as read (N) and to quickly scroll up or down by 10 items (I and U).

Lots of other changes and improvements:

  • Improved performance when updating feeds.
  • In Firefox 4 the number of unread items can be shown on the toolbar button, now that the status bar is gone.
  • Items are opened in new tabs by default.
  • Some keyboard shortcuts have been rearranged.
  • Reorganized options window.
  • Trash folder doesn't show the number of unread items.

Fixed bugs:

  • Sliding notification about new items doesn't work.
  • When a feed is being updated its icon goes blank.
  • Items aren't deleted properly when Trash is emptied and keep taking up space on disk.
